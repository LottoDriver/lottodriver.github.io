Comprehensive Worldwide Lottery Coverage

LottoDriver, currently supports 116 lottery games in over 30 countries. No other lottery software provides such extensive coverage of lottery games in terms of offers and results!

The lottery games offers and results for the supported games can be easily integrated into your current software solution using simple API.

Benefits

Speed

With LottoDriver API in place results will be deliverd faster compared to your rivals. With faster results your customers will be happy.

Developer friendly

LottoDriver provides out-of-the-box SDK, distributed via NuGet for faster integration with your existing platform using fully functional .NET Framework and .NET Core examples written in C#. Only missing part is your small peace of code which will integrate LottoDriver with your platform.

Simplicity

Using provided SDK with full working code examples, you will get Lotto Draws and Results in your system without any complications.

Precision/Correctness

You can relay on data provided by LottoDriver because LottoDriver takse care of getting Results from multiple sources for you. Results are confirmed as valid after comparsion of Offical Lotto source data and two or more different sources. LottoDriver also detects change in already published results and this information is provieded to you so you can decide what to do in situations like this.

Service availability

LottoDriver Scrappers machines are running in Azure and they are distributed all over the globe in Azure Data Cernters. This will ensure that LottoDriver is feed with data without interupption. Also, with LottoDriver, scrapping pages is not your obligation anymore and you can rest assured that LottoDriver team will support you in changes of pages that are used as data sources for Draws and Results

Money Saving

With LottoDriver you can use your workers for smarter things. You don't need to waste your workers energy and time on results entering and validation.
